# Path: ops-analyst

Curated skills for an operations analyst. Install these on day one.

## Recommended plugins

- **ops-essentials** — Weekly updates and meeting prep are 80% of the job nobody trains you for. Use the weekly-status-update skill every Friday — it forces outcomes-not-activity, which is the single biggest improvement you can make to how you communicate with leadership.
- **customer-research** — Even if you're not on the research team, you'll be pulled into customer conversations. The interview-prep skill makes you useful in a call you weren't supposed to lead.

## Why only two plugins?

Fewer is better. The Ramp data point that built Dewey: people who install one skill on day one and immediately get a result are the ones who stick. Two well-chosen plugins beat eight you never open.

If you find yourself wanting more, run `/dewey install` and browse the full marketplace — but only add what solves a problem you have *this week*.
