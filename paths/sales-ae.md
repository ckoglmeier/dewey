# Path: sales-ae

Curated skills for a new Account Executive. Install these on day one.

## Recommended plugins

- **competitive-intelligence** — Deals stall when AEs can't articulate one specific way we differ from incumbents. Use this before any call where a competitor is in the deal.
- **customer-research** — Discovery is the highest-leverage skill in sales. The interview-prep skill turns 5 minutes of prep into a meaningfully better call.
- **sales-enablement** — Follow-ups are where deals are made or lost between meetings. Send a recap after every call. Use the stakeholder-followup skill so the recap actually moves the deal.
- **ops-essentials** — Meeting prep and weekly updates aren't sales-specific, but every AE needs them. Skip the weekly-update skill if your team doesn't ask for one.

## How to use this path

When you run `/classroom` and tell it you're a new AE, the Guide will offer to install all four of these. Say yes, then immediately try `/competitive-analysis` on a competitor in your current pipeline. The wow happens when you get a real brief in 90 seconds.
