## Recommended plugins

- **competitive-intelligence** — Deals stall when reps can't articulate one specific way {{ORG_NAME}} differs from incumbents. Use this before any call where a competitor is in the deal.
- **customer-research** — Discovery is the highest-leverage skill in sales. The interview-prep skill turns 5 minutes of prep into a meaningfully better call.

- **ops-essentials** — Meeting prep and weekly updates aren't sales-specific, but every AE needs them. Skip the weekly-update skill if your team doesn't ask for one.
